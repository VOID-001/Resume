The summary of last 5 episodes ...

Episode 987 - The battle against Kaido and his forces continues. Luffy and the other members of the Straw Hat Pirates are locked in combat with various opponents. In this episode, Luffy faces off against Ulti and Page One, two members of the Beast Pirates, while continuing his quest to find and rescue his friend, Tama.

Episode 988 - The battle on Onigashima intensifies as Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates clash with the Beast Pirates and the Numbers. Meanwhile, the Nine Red Scabbards continue their assault on Kaido. The episode also delves into the past and the relationship between Oden Kozuki and the traitorous member of the Nine Red Scabbards, Kanjuro.

Episode 989 - The battle rages on as the Straw Hat Pirates and their allies make their move against the Beast Pirates. The episode explores the backstory of Yamato, Kaido's daughter, and her desire to break free from her father's control. The fight between the Tobi Roppo and the Straw Hat Pirates escalates.

Episode 990 - The battle continues to escalate as the Straw Hat Pirates, the Nine Red Scabbards, and their allies fight against the Beast Pirates and Kaido's forces. We see various clashes and strategies being employed by both sides. Additionally, the past of Ace and his connection to Wano is revealed in this episode.

Episode 991 - The battle on Onigashima reaches a critical point. The Straw Hat Pirates and their allies are faced with formidable opponents, including the Tobi Roppo and the Beast Pirates' elite forces. Luffy and Zoro continue their respective fights, and the episode unfolds more about the history of the Kozuki clan and the significance of the Poneglyphs.
